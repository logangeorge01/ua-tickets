How it works
When someone wants to buy your ticket, they will Venmo us the amount you specified. We will then hide your ticket listing from the site and text you to transfer your ticket to our account within 15 minutes. Once you've done so, we will send you the money and transfer the ticket to the buyer. If we do not receive your ticket in 15 minutes, you will receive a strike. For strikes 1 and 2, your ticket listing will reappear on the site. On strike 3, your listing will be removed from the site and you won't be able to create a new listing until next week's game. This process ensures that transactions occur quickly and without dispute, as well as preventing students from selling tickets they do not have.
